About IIMBG Untold
A student-led storytelling platform for the IIM Bodh Gaya community

IIMBG Untold is a student-led storytelling platform created for the Indian Institute of Management Bodh Gaya community.

Every campus has stories that never make it to social media or official newsletters — the late-night hostel conversations, the struggles before exams, the excitement of cultural fests, internship journeys, placement experiences, friendships, failures, and moments that shape us.

This platform exists to preserve those stories.

Whether you're a current student, an alumnus, faculty member, or staff member, IIMBG Untold is your space to write, read, and connect with the community through authentic experiences.

Our mission is simple: To build a digital archive of the people, memories, and moments that make IIM Bodh Gaya special.

Who can write?

Current Students Alumni Faculty Members Staff Members Exchange Students Guest Contributors (with approval)

Community Guidelines
IIMBG Untold is built on trust — please help us keep it welcoming

We encourage

Honest storytelling Constructive discussions Respectful opinions Helpful advice Creativity Authentic experiences

Please avoid

Contribute
Tell Your Story
Everyone at IIM Bodh Gaya has a story worth sharing

Whether it's your first day on campus, a memorable fest, an internship experience, a placement journey, or simply a lesson life taught you — we'd love to hear it.

Before submitting

  • Write from your own experience.
  • Keep your content respectful.
  • Use a clear title.
  • Add images if they enhance your story.
  • Proofread before publishing.

Quality matters more than length. Some exceptional stories may also be featured on the homepage. Your story could become someone else's inspiration.
